The Senior Ball
May 05, 2018
07:30-11:00PM

About



Senior Ball will be held at Boston Park Plaza on Saturday, May 5th, 2018, from 7:30pm to 11:00pm. Please bring your MIT Student ID and a government-issued photo ID or passport. Black tie attire is requested.

Senior Ball tickets may be purchased online starting on Monday, April 9th at 10:00am and ending on Monday, April 30th at 10:00pm. Please bring an electronic or printed copy of your ticket to be scanned at the entrance.

The price of one Senior Ball ticket is $50.

Members of the MIT Class of 2018 can purchase up to two Senior Ball tickets. Each Senior Ball ticket comes with 2 drink tickets; each drink ticket may be exchanged for 1 alcoholic drink or 2 non-alcoholic drinks. Guests must be over 21-years-old as of May 5th, 2018 to exchange drink tickets for alcoholic drinks.

Refunds can be made through adMIT ONE before Monday, April 30th at 10:00pm.

Ticketing



MIT students with ‘year 4’ certificates may purchase tickets online through adMIT ONE. This includes students who are part of the MIT Class of 2018 and students on the June 2018 degree list. If you have issues with certificates, please email tickets@mit.edu.

You can bring anyone you’d like as your guest. They do not need to be from the MIT Class of 2018. You do not need to know who your guest is when you are purchasing tickets.

Logistics



Boston Park Plaza is located at 34 Columbus Avenue, Boston, MA 02116. The doors will open at 7:30pm and close at 9:00pm. Admission will not be allowed after 9:00pm. This is a strict cut-off, so please plan accordingly -- no exceptions. Additionally, if your guest is not part of the MIT Class of 2018, then they must arrive with you.

There will be a free coat check. However, all bags will be searched at the entrance. Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside and will be confiscated at the door.

Refreshments



There will be many hors d'oeuvres, small plates, and desserts served throughout the event. We suggest eating dinner beforehand. There will be many vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free food options. There will not be any halal or kosher options. If you have any specific questions about the food and drinks, please email us at seniorball2018@mit.edu.

Each Senior Ball ticket comes with 2 drink tickets; each drink ticket may be exchanged for 1 alcoholic drink or 2 non-alcoholic drinks. Guests must be over 21-years-old as of May 5th, 2018 to exchange drink tickets for alcoholic drinks. In addition, you will be able to purchase drinks with cash and credit/debit cards.

Attire



Black-tie attire is requested. Tuxedo and gown rentals will be available on campus.

The website will be updated with more information about the rental information after Spring Break.
