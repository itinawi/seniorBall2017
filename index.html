Senior Ball
May 05, 2018
7:30-11:00PM

About

Senior Ball will be held at Boston Park Plaza on Saturday, May 5th, 2018, from 7:30pm to 11:00pm. Please bring your MIT Student ID and a government-issued photo ID or passport. Black tie attire is requested.

Senior Ball tickets may be purchased online starting on Monday, April 9th at 10:00am and ending on Monday, April 30th at 10:00pm. Please bring an electronic or printed copy of your ticket to be scanned at the entrance.

The price of one Senior Ball ticket is $50.

Members of the MIT Class of 2018 can purchase up to two Senior Ball tickets. Each Senior Ball ticket comes with 2 drink tickets; each drink ticket may be exchanged for 1 alcoholic drink or 2 non-alcoholic drinks. Guests must be over 21-years-old as of May 5th, 2018 to exchange drink tickets for alcoholic drinks.

Refunds can be made through adMIT ONE before Monday, April 30th at 10:00pm.

FAQ

How do I get to the event?
Boston Park Plaza is located at 34 Columbus Avenue, Boston, MA 02116.

When should I arrive?
Doors open at 7:30pm and close at 9:00pm. Admission will not be allowed after 9:00pm. This is a strict cut-off, so please plan accordingly -- no exceptions.

Who can purchase tickets?
MIT students may purchase tickets online through adMIT ONE. However, only students who are part of the MIT Class of 2018 and students on the June 2018 degree list (and their guests) will be admitted at the event.

If you have issues with certificates, please email tickets@mit.edu.

Who can I bring as my guest?
Anyone! Your guest does not need to be from the MIT Class of 2018.

Can I buy a guest ticket even if I don’t know who I’m bringing yet?
Yes, of course. You do not need to know who your guest is when you are purchasing tickets.

Do I have to arrive with my guest?
If your guest is unable to purchase their own ticket, then they must arrive with you. In other words, if they are not part of the MIT Class of 2018 or on the June 2018 degree list, then they must arrive with you.

What should I wear?
Black-tie attire is requested. Tuxedo and gown rentals will be available on campus; more details are coming soon.

Will there be a coat check?
All bags will be searched at the entrance. Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside and will be confiscated at the door. There will be a free coat check.

What type of food will there be?
There will be many hors d'oeuvres, small plates, and desserts served throughout the event. We suggest eating dinner beforehand.

Will there be a cash bar?
Yes. In addition to the 2 drink tickets, you will be able to purchase drinks with cash and credit/debit cards.

Are you accommodating any dietary restrictions?
Yes. There will be many vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free food options. There will not be any kosher options. If you have any specific questions about the food and drinks, please email mingshi@mit.edu.
